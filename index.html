Half Sword Game Guide and News

Welcome to the world of Half Sword! This immersive medieval combat simulation game is based on historical accuracy, where players start as commoners and progress to become knights, experiencing brutal duels of the 15th-century Europe.

Game Features

  • Realistic Weapons and Armor: The game offers historically accurate weapons and armor, allowing players to dive deep into a rich medieval world and experience diverse, distinctive equipment.
  • Innovative Combat Mechanics: Players control their weapons precisely through mouse movements, feeling the tension of every collision. All animations and actions are meticulously designed for realistic combat.
  • HEMA-based Combat System: The combat system is based on feedback from European Historical Martial Arts (HEMA) practitioners and swordfighters, enabling players to experience the intricacies of swordsmanship.

Gameplay

In Half Sword, players participate in various duels and tournaments, defeating opponents and earning the title of arena champion. As your rank increases, you'll face more formidable enemies and improve your battle tactics and strategies.

Tips and Strategy

  • Master Weapon Control: Use mouse movements to control the sword, mastering different weapons' characteristics to find the fighting style that suits you best.
